About Us

Michael Baker is a Fitness and Physical Preparation Specialist with a mission to guide adults and athletes to become the best version of themselves through fitness and performance training.

Mike holds a B.S. in Exercise Science from Bloomsburg University. He is a Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist through the NSCA and is FMS Level 1 certified. Mike has nearly 10,000 hours of training experience and has worked with hundreds of clients of ranging from 9-90 years old, including over 100 division 1 female and male athletes, dozens of NFL veterans and prospects, and his own mother.
